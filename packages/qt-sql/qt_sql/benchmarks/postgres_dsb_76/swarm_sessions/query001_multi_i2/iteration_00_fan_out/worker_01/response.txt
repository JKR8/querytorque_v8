## Part 1: Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
├── [CTE] filtered_store_returns  [+]  (decomposed from original CTE)
│   ├── SCAN (store_returns, date_dim (join))
│   ├── JOIN (sr_returned_date_sk = d_date_sk)
│   ├── FILTER (d_year = 2000)
│   ├── FILTER (sr_return_amt / sr_return_quantity BETWEEN 115 AND 174)
│   └── OUTPUT (sr_customer_sk, sr_store_sk, sr_reason_sk, SR_RETURN_AMT_INC_TAX)
├── [CTE] store_averages  [+]  (decorrelated from subquery)
│   ├── SCAN (filtered_store_returns)
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY sr_store_sk)
│   ├── AGG (AVG(SR_RETURN_AMT_INC_TAX) * 1.2)
│   └── OUTPUT (sr_store_sk, store_avg_threshold)
├── [CTE] customer_returns  [+]  (decomposed from original CTE)
│   ├── SCAN (filtered_store_returns)
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY sr_customer_sk, sr_store_sk, sr_reason_sk)
│   ├── AGG (SUM(SR_RETURN_AMT_INC_TAX))
│   └── OUTPUT (ctr_customer_sk, ctr_store_sk, ctr_reason_sk, ctr_total_return)
└── [MAIN] main_query  [~]  (replaces correlated subquery with CTE join)
    ├── SCAN (customer_returns, store_averages, store, customer, customer_demographics)
    ├── JOIN (customer_returns.ctr_store_sk = store_averages.sr_store_sk)
    ├── JOIN (store.s_store_sk = customer_returns.ctr_store_sk)
    ├── JOIN (customer.c_customer_sk = customer_returns.ctr_customer_sk)
    ├── JOIN (customer_demographics.cd_demo_sk = customer.c_current_cdemo_sk)
    ├── FILTER (customer_returns.ctr_total_return > store_averages.store_avg_threshold)
    ├── FILTER (customer_returns.ctr_reason_sk BETWEEN 17 AND 20)
    ├── FILTER (store.s_state IN ('IA', 'KY', 'NE'))
    ├── FILTER (customer_demographics.cd_marital_status IN ('S', 'S'))
    ├── FILTER (customer_demographics.cd_education_status IN ('4 yr Degree', '4 yr Degree'))
    ├── FILTER (customer_demographics.cd_gender = 'M')
    ├── FILTER (customer.c_birth_month = 4)
    ├── FILTER (customer.c_birth_year BETWEEN 1987 AND 1993)
    ├── SORT (c_customer_id ASC)
    └── OUTPUT (c_customer_id)
```
