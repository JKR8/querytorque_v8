## Part 1: Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
├── [CTE] date_prefilter [~] Changed: Pre-filter date_dim to 2002 only, explicit JOIN syntax
│   ├── SCAN (date_dim)
│   ├── FILTER (d_year = 2002)
│   └── OUTPUT (d_date_sk)
├── [CTE] addr_prefilter [~] Changed: Explicit CTE for all customer_address rows
│   ├── SCAN (customer_address)
│   └── OUTPUT (ca_address_sk, ca_state)
├── [CTE] fact_agg [~] Changed: Convert comma joins to explicit INNER JOIN, use prefilter CTEs
│   ├── SCAN (catalog_returns)
│   ├── JOIN (INNER) date_prefilter ON cr_returned_date_sk = d_date_sk
│   ├── JOIN (INNER) addr_prefilter ON cr_returning_addr_sk = ca_address_sk
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY cr_returning_customer_sk, ca_state)
│   └── OUTPUT (ctr_customer_sk, ctr_state, ctr_total_return)
├── [CTE] state_avg [~] Changed: Materialize per-state thresholds from fact_agg
│   ├── SCAN (fact_agg)
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY ctr_state)
│   └── OUTPUT (ctr_state, state_avg_threshold)
└── [MAIN] main_explicit_joins [~] Changed: Replace correlated subquery with state_avg CTE, explicit JOIN syntax
    ├── SCAN (fact_agg AS ctr1, state_avg, customer, customer_address)
    ├── JOIN (INNER) state_avg ON ctr1.ctr_state = state_avg.ctr_state
    ├── JOIN (INNER) customer ON ctr1.ctr_customer_sk = c_customer_sk
    ├── JOIN (INNER) customer_address ON ca_address_sk = c_current_addr_sk
    ├── FILTER (ca_state = 'OH' AND ctr_total_return > state_avg_threshold)
    ├── SORT (original 16-column ORDER BY)
    └── OUTPUT (original 16 columns)
```

Changes: Isolated date_dim filtering into a CTE, converted all comma joins to explicit INNER JOIN syntax, and replaced the correlated subquery with a materialized state averages CTE. This eliminates per-state re-execution and improves PostgreSQL's cardinality estimation.

Expected speedup: 2-3x due to elimination of correlated subquery and better join planning with pre-filtered dimension CTEs.
