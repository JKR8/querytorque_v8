## Part 1: Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
├── [CTE] date_filter_cte  [+]  Cost: ~1%  Rows: ~365
│   └── SCAN (date_dim) with FILTER (d_year = 2002)
├── [CTE] returning_addr_cte  [+]  Cost: ~1%  Rows: all customer_address
│   └── SCAN (customer_address) with no filter
├── [CTE] fact_agg_cte  [~]  Cost: ~40%  Rows: ~1K
│   ├── JOIN (catalog_returns × date_filter_cte) on cr_returned_date_sk = d_date_sk
│   ├── JOIN (× returning_addr_cte) on cr_returning_addr_sk = ca_address_sk
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY cr_returning_customer_sk, ca_state) with SUM(cr_return_amt_inc_tax)
│   └── OUTPUT (ctr_customer_sk, ctr_state, ctr_total_return)
├── [CTE] state_avg_cte  [+]  Cost: ~10%  Rows: distinct states
│   ├── SCAN (fact_agg_cte)
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY ctr_state) with AVG(ctr_total_return)*1.2
│   └── OUTPUT (ctr_state, state_avg_threshold)
└── [MAIN] main_join  [~]  Cost: ~48%  Rows: 100
    ├── JOIN (fact_agg_cte × state_avg_cte) on ctr_state equality
    ├── JOIN (× customer) on ctr_customer_sk = c_customer_sk
    ├── JOIN (× customer_address) on ca_address_sk = c_current_addr_sk
    ├── FILTER (ca_state = 'OH' AND ctr_total_return > state_avg_threshold)
    ├── SORT (original 16-column ORDER BY)
    └── OUTPUT (original 16 columns)
```
