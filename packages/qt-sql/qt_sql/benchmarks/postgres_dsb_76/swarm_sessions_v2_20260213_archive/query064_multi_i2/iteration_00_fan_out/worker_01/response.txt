**Changes:** Restructured to follow target logical tree exactly - pre-filtered selective dimensions into CTEs (promotion, customer_address, customer_demographics), converted implicit joins to explicit INNER JOIN syntax, and decomposed the original cross_sales CTE into fact_join (joins without aggregation) and cross_sales_agg (aggregation). This preserves all semantic invariants including the critical `cd1.cd_marital_status <> cd2.cd_marital_status` filter.

**Expected speedup:** ~2-3x based on similar patterns (pg_dimension_prefetch_star showed 3.32x, pg_date_cte_explicit_join showed 2.28x). The main gains come from: 1) Early reduction of dimension tables to tiny hash tables, 2) Explicit join syntax giving optimizer better cardinality estimates, 3) Logical decomposition allowing better join ordering.
