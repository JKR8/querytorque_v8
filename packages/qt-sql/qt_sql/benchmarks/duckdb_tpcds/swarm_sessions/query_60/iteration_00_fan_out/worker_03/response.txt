## Part 1: Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
├── [CTE] filtered_date  [+]
│   ├── SCAN (date_dim)
│   ├── FILTER (d_year=2000, d_moy=8)
│   └── OUTPUT (d_date_sk)
├── [CTE] filtered_item  [+]
│   ├── SCAN (item)
│   ├── FILTER (i_category='Children')
│   └── OUTPUT (i_item_id, i_item_sk)
├── [CTE] filtered_ca  [+]
│   ├── SCAN (customer_address)
│   ├── FILTER (ca_gmt_offset=-7)
│   └── OUTPUT (ca_address_sk)
├── [CTE] prefetched_sales  [+]
│   ├── SCAN (store_sales) with IN subquery filters to filtered_item, filtered_date, filtered_ca
│   ├── SCAN (catalog_sales) with IN subquery filters to filtered_item, filtered_date, filtered_ca
│   ├── SCAN (web_sales) with IN subquery filters to filtered_item, filtered_date, filtered_ca
│   ├── UNION ALL (all three channel results)
│   └── OUTPUT (item_sk, date_sk, addr_sk, sales_price)
├── [CTE] main_aggregation  [+]
│   ├── JOIN (prefetched_sales ⋈ filtered_item ON item_sk = i_item_sk)
│   ├── JOIN (⋈ filtered_date ON date_sk = d_date_sk)
│   ├── JOIN (⋈ filtered_ca ON addr_sk = ca_address_sk)
│   ├── AGG (GROUP BY i_item_id, SUM(sales_price) AS total_sales)
│   ├── SORT (i_item_id ASC, total_sales ASC)
│   ├── LIMIT (100)
│   └── OUTPUT (i_item_id, total_sales)
└── [MAIN] main_query  [~]
    ├── SCAN (main_aggregation)
    └── OUTPUT (i_item_id, total_sales)
```

**Change markers:**
- `[+] filtered_date`: New CTE replacing repeated date_dim scans
- `[+] filtered_item`: New CTE replacing repeated item scans and IN subqueries  
- `[+] filtered_ca`: New CTE replacing repeated customer_address scans
- `[+] prefetched_sales`: New CTE unifying channel fact tables with early filtering
- `[+] main_aggregation`: New CTE performing final joins and aggregation
- `[~] main_query`: Simplified to SELECT from main_aggregation CTE
