## Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
├── [CTE] filtered_date  [+]
│   ├── SCAN date_dim
│   ├── FILTER d_year = 2002
│   └── OUTPUT d_date_sk
├── [CTE] filtered_return_address  [+]
│   ├── SCAN customer_address
│   └── OUTPUT ca_address_sk, return_state
├── [CTE] web_returns_join  [+]
│   ├── SCAN web_returns
│   ├── JOIN filtered_date (wr_returned_date_sk = d_date_sk)
│   ├── JOIN filtered_return_address (wr_returning_addr_sk = ca_address_sk)
│   └── OUTPUT wr_returning_customer_sk, return_state, wr_return_amt
├── [CTE] ctr_aggregate  [+]
│   ├── SCAN web_returns_join
│   ├── AGG GROUP BY wr_returning_customer_sk, return_state
│   ├── AGG SUM(wr_return_amt) AS ctr_total_return
│   └── OUTPUT ctr_customer_sk, ctr_state, ctr_total_return
├── [CTE] state_avg  [+]
│   ├── SCAN ctr_aggregate
│   ├── AGG GROUP BY ctr_state
│   ├── AGG AVG(ctr_total_return) * 1.2 AS avg_state_return
│   └── OUTPUT ctr_state, avg_state_return
├── [CTE] filtered_customer  [+]
│   ├── SCAN customer
│   ├── SCAN customer_address
│   ├── JOIN c_current_addr_sk = ca_address_sk
│   ├── FILTER ca_state = 'IN'
│   └── OUTPUT c_customer_sk, c_customer_id, c_salutation, c_first_name, c_last_name, c_preferred_cust_flag, c_birth_day, c_birth_month, c_birth_year, c_birth_country, c_login, c_email_address, c_last_review_date_sk
├── [DERIVED] main_join  [+]
│   ├── SCAN filtered_customer
│   ├── SCAN ctr_aggregate AS ctr1
│   ├── SCAN state_avg
│   ├── JOIN filtered_customer.c_customer_sk = ctr1.ctr_customer_sk
│   ├── JOIN ctr1.ctr_state = state_avg.ctr_state
│   ├── FILTER ctr1.ctr_total_return > state_avg.avg_state_return
│   └── OUTPUT c_customer_id, c_salutation, c_first_name, c_last_name, c_preferred_cust_flag, c_birth_day, c_birth_month, c_birth_year, c_birth_country, c_login, c_email_address, c_last_review_date_sk, ctr_total_return
└── [MAIN] top_n  [~]
    ├── SCAN main_join
    ├── SORT (13 columns)
    ├── LIMIT 100
    └── OUTPUT (same as main_join)
```

**Changes**: Replaced single CTE with staged pipeline: pre-filter date and return address dimensions, join with fact table, compute aggregates, then join with filtered customers and precomputed state averages. Eliminated correlated subquery.

**Expected speedup**: ~3x-4x from dimension pre-filtering and decorrelation, avoiding repeated per-state average calculations and enabling early fact table reduction.
