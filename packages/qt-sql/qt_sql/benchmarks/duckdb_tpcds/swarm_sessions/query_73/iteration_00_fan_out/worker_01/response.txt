### Part 1: Modified Logic Tree

```
QUERY: (single statement)
└── [MAIN] main_query  [~]  — Restructured into CTE chain matching target logical tree
    ├── [CTE] filtered_date  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (date_dim)
    │   └── FILTER (d_dom BETWEEN 1 AND 2 AND d_year IN (2000,2001,2002))
    ├── [CTE] filtered_store  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (store)
    │   └── FILTER (s_county IN (...))
    ├── [CTE] filtered_hd  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (household_demographics)
    │   └── FILTER (hd_buy_potential IN (...) AND hd_vehicle_count > 0 AND CASE > 1)
    ├── [CTE] prefetched_sales  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (store_sales)
    │   ├── JOIN filtered_date (INNER ON ss_sold_date_sk = d_date_sk)
    │   ├── JOIN filtered_store (INNER ON ss_store_sk = s_store_sk)
    │   └── JOIN filtered_hd (INNER ON ss_hdemo_sk = hd_demo_sk)
    ├── [CTE] aggregated_sales  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (prefetched_sales)
    │   └── AGG (GROUP BY ss_ticket_number, ss_customer_sk; COUNT(*) AS cnt)
    ├── [CTE] filtered_counts  [+]
    │   ├── SCAN (aggregated_sales)
    │   └── FILTER (cnt BETWEEN 1 AND 5)
    └── [SELECT] final_result  [~]
        ├── SCAN (filtered_counts)
        ├── JOIN customer (INNER ON ss_customer_sk = c_customer_sk)
        ├── SORT (cnt DESC, c_last_name ASC)
        └── OUTPUT (c_last_name, c_first_name, c_salutation, c_preferred_cust_flag, ss_ticket_number, cnt)
```

Changes: Restructured into a staged CTE pipeline that prefilters three dimension tables (date_dim, store, household_demographics) before joining with the fact table (store_sales), then aggregates and filters counts before the final customer join. This isolates dimension filters into small hash tables and progressively reduces data volume through the pipeline.

Expected speedup: ~2–3× from multi-dimension prefetch and dimension isolation patterns, as each dimension CTE is highly selective and reduces the fact scan cardinality before joins.
