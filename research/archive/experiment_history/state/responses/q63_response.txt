Looking at the subgraph slice, I see an opportunity to apply **early_filter** by moving the date dimension filter earlier in the computation. Currently, `first_branch` and `second_branch` are joining the large fact table (`store_sales`) with filtered dates, but we can create a smaller filtered fact table first.
