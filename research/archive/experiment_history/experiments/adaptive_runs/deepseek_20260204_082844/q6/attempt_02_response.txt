Looking at the query and execution plan, I can see two correlated subqueries that are causing performance issues:

1. `d.d_month_seq = (SELECT DISTINCT (d_month_seq) FROM date_dim WHERE d_year = 2002 AND d_moy = 3)` - This is executed once
2. `i.i_current_price > 1.2 * (SELECT AVG(j.i_current_price) FROM item AS j WHERE j.i_category = i.i_category)` - This is correlated and executed for each row

The execution plan shows a complex LEFT_DELIM_JOIN for the correlated subquery, which is expensive. I'll use the **decorrelate** transform to handle the correlated subquery and **early_filter** for the date dimension.
