Looking at this query, I can see two main optimization opportunities:

1. **Date CTE Isolation**: The date_dim table is filtered by `d_year = 1998 AND d_moy = 11`, which reduces from ~73K rows to just 30 rows. Creating a CTE for filtered dates will dramatically reduce the join size early.

2. **OR_TO_UNION**: The complex OR condition on customer_demographics can be split into UNION ALL branches, allowing better filtering and potential index usage.
